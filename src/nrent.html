Gross median rent

Gross rent is the contract rent plus the estimated average monthly cost of utilities and fuels.

In 2010, Morningside Heights had an average gross median rent of $1,423, while Manhattanville’s average was $757 and South and Central Harlem’s average was $849.

In 2015, Morningside Heights had an average gross median rent of $1,825, while Manhattanville’s average was $957 and South and Central Harlem’s average was $1,069.

In 2019, Morningside Heights had an average gross median rent of $2,030, while Manhattanville’s average was $1,136 and South and Central Harlem’s average was $1,394.
